How To Play

THE OBJECTIVE OF THIS GAME IS TO GUESS YOUR STARTING LOCATION ON THE MINIMAP BY LOOKING AROUND YOUR SURROUNDINGS.

KEEP IN MIND THAT YOU ARE SUPPOSED TO GUESS YOUR "STARTING" LOCATION, AND NOT YOUR "CURRENT" POSITION!

GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!

---------------------------------------

Controls

Move : WASD
Look Around : Drag
FOV Control : Wheel Up/Down
Map Zoom In/Out : Wheel Up/Down On Map
Change Map Size : Click +/- Button
Guess Your Born Place : Click Map
Confirm Your : Click Guess Button
Back To Born Place : Click H Button
